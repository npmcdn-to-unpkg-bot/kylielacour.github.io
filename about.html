ABOUT PRINTWELL

Printwell.club is Portland State University's collaborative print & prepress guide for students. The project aims to connect designers and printers, to educate students about the finer details of prepress techniques, and to ultimately bridge the gap between them.

After graduation (in the "real world") prepress knowledge is invaluable and can help students get hired and stay hired.
